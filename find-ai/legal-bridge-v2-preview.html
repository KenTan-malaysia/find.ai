Your Compliance Roadmap
Manufacturing in Johor (agricultural land)
←
⚠️ CONDITIONALLY YES
You CAN do this — but you need land conversion first. Agricultural land cannot be used for manufacturing without State Authority approval.
🇨🇳 China Assumption Trap
In China, land use change (土地用途变更) is a standard process with clear timelines. In Malaysia, it requires State Authority approval and can take 12-24 months with NO guarantee of success. Do NOT sign lease until conversion is confirmed.
REQUIRED Must have before operating
1
Land Use Conversion
→ Pejabat Tanah Johor (Land Office)
Convert agricultural → industrial under NLC Section 124. Must be done BEFORE any building or operations.
⏱ 12-24 months 💰 RM50K-200K premium ⚠️ May be rejected
2
DOE Environmental License
→ Jabatan Alam Sekitar (DOE)
Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) required for manufacturing. Apply to DOE Johor. Scheduled waste handling needs separate registration.
⏱ 3-6 months 💰 RM5K-50K (EIA)
3
Factory & Machinery Registration
→ DOSH (Jabatan Keselamatan & Kesihatan)
Register under Factories & Machinery Act 1967. Boiler/pressure vessel certs if applicable. OSHA compliance audit.
⏱ 1-3 months 💰 RM1K-5K
4
PBT Business License
→ Majlis Perbandaran / Daerah Johor
Local council business premise license. Annual renewal. Must match approved land use.
⏱ 2-4 weeks 💰 RM200-2K/year
5
Fire Certificate (CF)
→ Bomba (Fire & Rescue Dept)
Fire safety cert required for factory operations. Inspection of fire exits, extinguishers, suppression systems.
⏱ 2-6 weeks 💰 RM500-3K
6
Foreign Worker Permits
→ Imigresen Malaysia + MOHR
Apply through FWCMS system. Need: levy payment, SOCSO registration, workers' housing compliance (Act 446). Quota approval from MOHR.
⏱ 2-6 months 💰 RM1.8K-2.5K/worker/year
RECOMMENDED Strongly advised
7
MIDA Manufacturing License
→ MIDA (Investment Authority)
If capital > RM2.5M or 75+ workers. Unlocks tax incentives (Pioneer Status / ITA). Strongly recommended for foreign companies.
⏱ 2-4 months 💰 Free to apply
8
Environmental Liability Insurance
→ Any licensed insurer
EQA 1974 holds landlord AND tenant liable. Insurance protects both parties from environmental claims and cleanup costs.
💰 RM3K-15K/year
Total licenses needed 6 required + 2 recommended
Estimated total time 14-30 months (land conversion is bottleneck)
Estimated total cost RM60K - RM275K+
Advisory guide only. Requirements may vary by local authority. Consult a licensed advisor before proceeding.

Buy a condo in Penang (foreigner)
←
⚠️ CONDITIONALLY YES
Foreigners CAN buy in Penang — but minimum RM1,000,000 (island) or RM500,000 (mainland). You also need State Authority consent.
🇨🇳 China Assumption Trap
In China, property purchase is straightforward once you have funds. No "定金 double return" (earnest money) protection exists in Malaysia — if you default, you simply lose your deposit. Also: no 优先购买权 (right of first refusal) for existing tenants.
REQUIRED Must complete
1
Verify Minimum Price Threshold
→ Penang State Government
Island: RM1,000,000 min. Mainland: RM500,000 min. Property MUST be above threshold — no exceptions for foreigners.
⚠️ Below threshold = rejected
2
State Authority Consent
→ Pejabat Tanah & Galian Pulau Pinang
Apply via lawyer. Foreign ownership must be approved by State Authority under NLC. Non-strata titles are harder.
⏱ 3-6 months 💰 RM1K-3K (legal fees)
3
Stamp Duty on SPA
→ LHDN (IRB) via MyTax
Tiered: 1% (first RM100K), 2% (RM100K-500K), 3% (RM500K-1M), 4% (above RM1M). SDSAS self-assessment applies.
💰 RM24,000+ (on RM1M property) ⏱ Within 30 days
4
Legal Fees & Disbursements
→ Licensed Malaysian lawyer
SPA legal fees (tiered scale), loan agreement fees (if financing), title transfer fees, registration fees.
💰 RM10K-25K total
5
RPGT Registration
→ LHDN (IRB)
Real Property Gains Tax: foreigners pay 30% (first 5 years), 10% (after year 5). File SPA with LHDN within 60 days of disposal.
⚠️ 30% if sold within 5 years
RECOMMENDED Strongly advised
6
Land Title Search
→ Pejabat Tanah
Verify: freehold/leasehold, caveats, charges, Malay Reserve status, encumbrances. CRITICAL — do this before paying anything.
💰 RM50-100 ⏱ Same day
7
Defect Inspection (Sub-sale)
→ Licensed building inspector
In China you have 质保期 (warranty period). In Malaysia, developer warranty is only 24 months from VP. For sub-sale, inspect BEFORE signing SPA.
💰 RM500-1.5K
📅 Typical Timeline
Week 1-2: Sign SPA + pay 10% deposit
Lawyer prepares SPA. You pay booking + deposit.
Month 1-3: State Authority consent
Application submitted. Wait for approval. ⚠️ If rejected, SPA is void.
Month 3-4: Stamp duty + balance payment
Pay stamp duty via SDSAS. Complete balance 90% (or bank loan disbursement).
Month 4-6: Title transfer registered
Title transferred at Land Office. Keys handed over. Done! 🎉
Total steps 5 required + 2 recommended
Estimated timeline 4-6 months
Estimated total cost (excl. property) RM35K - RM55K+
Advisory guide only. Requirements vary by state and property type. Consult a licensed lawyer before proceeding.
