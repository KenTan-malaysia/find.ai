🇨🇳 CHINA

Broad right to exit for "fundamental breach". Courts are liberal. Tenant can terminate if property has defects.

🇲🇾 MALAYSIA

No general exit right. Must follow exact termination clause in agreement. Wrongful termination = breach.

📋 Protective Clause

"Either party may terminate with [2] months written notice. Early termination without notice shall result in forfeiture of the security deposit. No other termination rights apply."

不可抗力
Force Majeure
CLASH
🇨🇳 CHINA

Automatic by law. Broadly interpreted. COVID, floods, policy changes all qualify. No need to write it in.

🇲🇾 MALAYSIA

Must be explicitly written in the contract. Courts interpret narrowly. If not in agreement, it doesn't exist.

📋 Protective Clause

"Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control including but not limited to: natural disasters, epidemics, government orders, or war. Notice must be given within [7] days of the event."
