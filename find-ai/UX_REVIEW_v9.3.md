# Find.ai / Cakap 2.0 — v9.3 UI/UX Review
**Simulated walkthrough · 30 users · 2026-04-23**

## DNA read
Adjacent-to-bullseye. Polish doesn't change the spine (**trust before signing**) but unblocks users who bounce off the surface before reaching the tools that build that trust. Weigh every fix below by how directly it shortens the path from *"what is this app?"* to *"I trust this [tenant | agreement | stamp amount] because Find.ai checked it."*

---

## Method
Walked 30 simulated Malaysian users through three screens in order:

1. **Welcome** — 👋 / "Hi there." / "Before you sign anything — let's make sure it's safe." / "Let's go →"
2. **Pick** — 2 tiles (👤 Check a tenant / 💰 Calculate stamp duty) + Agreement Audit "Coming next" teaser strip + privacy lock chip.
3. **Persistent PeekChat dock** in its three states (56px bar → peek pane with last-3 preview → full chat escalation).

Each user had a real task (screen a tenant, review a draft, calculate SDSAS, or just browse).

**Persona mix** (Phase 1 target):
- 18 landlords (1-10 units, mostly first-timers or accidental)
- 7 property agents
- 5 SME commercial tenants

**Language split:** 12 EN-primary · 9 BM-primary · 7 ZH-primary · 2 Tamil-primary (not supported — worth noting).

**Device split:** 14 Android mid-to-budget · 12 iPhone · 4 tablet.

**Scored on three things per user:** (a) understood what Find.ai is in ≤5 seconds, (b) reached their target tool without getting stuck, (c) UI felt trustworthy.

## Thematic synthesis

### ✅ Top 5 wins (keep these)

1. **Two-tile Pick screen is a brag-reel demo** — agents (U19, U20) said it sells itself. Do not add tiles for tile's sake.
2. **"No sign-up" fine print** — U2, U15 both cited this as a trust signal. Keep it near the primary CTA.
3. **Dock as ambient help** — U17, U30, U19 found the "always there" pattern reassuring once they understood it. Fix discoverability, keep the pattern.
4. **Warm Navy Trust palette** — U4 *"cleanest property page"*, U6 *"feels expensive."* The palette is earning its keep.
5. **Haptic + animation feedback** — noted subtly by U14, U17. Tactile interactions reinforce trust.

### ⚠️ Top 8 issues (frequency-ranked)

1. **Dock purpose unclear on first sight (8/30)** — first-timers read it as a search bar, a WhatsApp imposter, or decoration.
   **Fix:** one-time ghost label ("Quick questions? Tap here") that fades after 3s or on first tap, plus subtle mic-wave hint.

2. **Empty peek preview looks broken (6/30)** — users with no chat history see a blank "Recent" strip.
   **Fix:** show 3 example prompts as ghost rows ("Can I trust this tenant?" / "How much stamp duty?" / "Is this deposit clause fair?").

3. **"Don't sign blind" tagline not on Welcome (5/30)** — strongest value-prop line lives in CLAUDE.md but not in the UI.
   **Fix:** add as small-caps mono brand motto under the Brand bar on Welcome.

4. **Font sizes too small for older users (4/30)** — 9px privacy chip, 11px lang toggle lose users over 55.
   **Fix:** bump privacy chip to 11px and lang toggle to 13px. Tap targets ≥ 32px.

5. **Tile ambiguity — commercial / company / foreign tenants (4/30)** — copy doesn't tell landlords "foreign tenants covered" or SME tenants "commercial lease covered."
   **Fix:** 1-line eyebrow above each tile — *"Residential + commercial"* / *"Individuals + companies"*.

6. **Audit teaser lacks a date or notify-me (4/30)** — dangling "Coming next" without timeline feels like empty promise.
   **Fix:** teaser strip becomes "Notify me when it ships" → captures email or phone → waitlist. Bonus: list-building for v3.3.3 launch.

7. **Tool modal close button unexpected (3/30)** — iOS users expect swipe-down or clear X top-left.
   **Fix:** top-left back chevron mirroring the landing back button; keep X top-right as secondary.

8. **Translation polish gaps — ZH awkward, Tamil missing (3/30)** — "让我们确保安全" reads translated; no Tamil at all.
   **Fix:** native-speaker ZH pass + Tamil policy decision (ship basic or add "Tamil coming" chip).

### Secondary observations (1+ user each)

- Dock mic icon should record on press-and-hold, not just expand (U17).
- Share-to-WhatsApp button missing from Welcome (U21 — high ROI for agent growth).
- Context chip "asking from Home" confusing for SME tenants (U29).
- Weak-signal SSE streaming untested (U25).
- iOS keyboard covers peek preview (U14).
- "Continue last case" button low discoverability (U16).
- Progress dots unlabeled — step 2 destination unclear (U3).
- Back button double-tap fires twice (U20).
- Dock flashes on Profile open before `peekHidden` kicks in (U20, ~200ms).
- Screening step 1 copy: whose IC? — tenant's, not yours (U18).
- Stamp tile should show "SDSAS 2026" for reassurance (U11).
- Welcome 👋 reads slightly "startup-y" — compliance product wants gravitas (U15).

## Overall read

**v9.3 is a solid, ship-able version.** The **persistent dock + two-tile pick + Warm Navy Trust palette** combination delivers on the positioning — *"this is a compliance toolkit, not a chatbot."* Agent-persona users (U19 especially) said it's demo-ready as-is.

**The biggest polish gaps are first-session discoverability** (dock, empty peek, missing tagline) and **age-accessibility** (font sizes, tap targets). Ship the five P1 tickets and the first-run clarity jumps noticeably **without changing any architecture.**

Under-served signals flagged for Phase 2/3 (not v9.3 polish):
- **Commercial / company tenants** (U5 dispute, U13 CN USCC, U28 SSM check) — Phase 3 CN-MY corridor.
- **Post-signing disputes** (U5 commercial dispute) — Phase 2 custodian layer.
- **Multi-tenancy HMO** (U12 student accommodation) — TOOL 2 Audit expansion.

These are not v9.3 polish — they're roadmap items already locked internally.
