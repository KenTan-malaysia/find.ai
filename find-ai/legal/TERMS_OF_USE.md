## 5. Tenant Credit Score and Trust Card

### 5.1 Nature of the Trust Score

The Find.ai Trust Score is a **support indicator** that summarizes available evidence about a tenant's previous paying behaviour. It is NOT:
- A guarantee of future behaviour
- A formal credit rating (such as CCRIS or CTOS)
- A recommendation to rent or not rent
- A substitute for the landlord's own due diligence

### 5.2 Methodology

The Trust Score combines:
- **Behaviour Score:** derived from utility bill payment timing patterns
- **Confidence Multiplier:** reflects the depth and quality of evidence provided

The exact algorithm is proprietary to Find.ai. Public-tier methodology disclosure is available at [HOW_TRUST_SCORE_WORKS link]. Find.ai reserves all rights to the methodology, including its expression, weights, and implementations.

### 5.3 Decision authority rests with landlord

### 5.4 Trust Card and Live Bound Verification

The Trust Card is a portable summary of a tenant's Trust Score, designed for sharing with prospective landlords. The QR code on the Trust Card triggers Live Bound Verification (LBV), which requires the tenant's live presence and face match. **The Trust Card alone is NOT sufficient proof of identity or score validity** — verification requires the LBV handshake.
